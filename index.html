About Pratishruti

Walking on the stairway made of clouds in the sky, Pratishruti just found its silver lining.

Pratishruti,Central India's largest cultural fest has developed into so much more.On this silver jubilee of Pratishruti, we present you a golden era of Folklore leaving you with platinum memories and a thousand diamond experiences.An era where you'll experience pure joy, where life lasting bonds will be created, where everyone is free from all the clutter that life hands us, where every expression is embraced and where every vibe takes you away on cloud nine

Four vibrant days ensuring a thrilling and remarkable journey showcasing hypnotic instances from diverse cultures await you.We decided to decorate the dates with the vibrant and diverse culture of India. They are going to knock on the doors of Pratishruti from 4th February to 7th February.

The Music Club has been ensuring to showcase their immense talent in front of the whole world by participating in TV shows such as Indian Idol, Sa Re Ga Ma Pa.
The Dance Club has made a trend to leave their mark in every competition they step into. Some of them are Laasya, Abhivyakti, Mood Indigo.
The Literary Club hosts many events throughout the year to improve verbal and non verbal skills of the students. Some of the events are Speak Out Loud, Abhivyakti, Youth Parliament etc.
The Drama Club has not only made its recognition in the college but outside as well by performing in the Indian Youth Conclave and hosting Role No. , Qirdaar , Naqaab etc.
The Photography Club covers all the events and happenings taking place in the college. They also organize workshops and seminars for the club members.

Students' Representative Council (SRC)

SRC (STUDENTS' REPRESENTATIVE COUNCIL) is a student body of the college consisting of student representatives from all the branches. It is the main student body of the college which organizes most of the events which include

Pratishrutia cultural extravaganza for colleges across Central India.
Gyanodaya an induction ceremony for the first year students
Aaruni the fresher’s night for the first years

and a string of various events that constitute the branch cup. All these activities throughout the year help
the students to improve on their communication skills, presentation skills, and organizational skills and help
boosting the self confidence of the students and help them explore their latent talents. The branch cup goes on for a
month consisting of Literary Cup, Dance Cup, Drama Cup, Music Cup and Photography cup.
This year we’ve introduced the Technical Cup as well.
